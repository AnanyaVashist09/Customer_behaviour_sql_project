Project Overview: Used a synthetic dataset to analyse customer shopping behaviour pattern for a year.

Dataset Overview: 
Customer ID – Unique identifier for each customer

Age – Age of the customer

Gender – Gender of the customer

Item Purchased – Product bought by the customer

Category – Product category (e.g., Clothing, Accessories)

Purchase Amount (USD) – Total amount spent in USD

Location – Customer’s geographic location

Size – Size of the purchased product

Color – Color of the purchased item

Season – Season in which purchase was made

Review Rating – Customer rating for the product

Subscription Status – Indicates if customer has a subscription

Shipping Type – Mode of delivery selected

Discount Applied – Whether discount was applied (Yes/No)

Promo Code Used – Indicates if promo code was used

Previous Purchases – Number of past purchases made

Payment Method – Mode of payment (Card, Cash, etc.)

Frequency of Purchases – How often customer makes purchases

Project Objectives:
This project is structured into four analytical areas, each addressing a key business function to deliver actionable insights and support data-driven decision-making.
1.Executive Focused (Strategic Overview):
-- Evaluate overall business performance across key KPIs
-- Monitor revenue trends, customer activity, and growth patterns
-- Provide a high-level summary to support leadership decision-making

2.Marketing Focused (Customer & Campaign Insights):
-- Identify high-value and target customer segments
-- Analyze effectiveness of campaigns, promotions, and discount strategies
-- Optimize marketing ROI while reducing dependency on discounts

3.Product Focused (Product Performance Analysis):
-- Identify top-performing products and categories driving revenue
-- Analyze customer demand patterns and purchase behavior
-- Evaluate discount dependency across products

4.Operations Focused (Efficiency & Process Improvement):
-- Assess operational efficiency and order processing trends
-- Identify potential risks and bottlenecks in operations
-- Recommend data-driven process improvements
